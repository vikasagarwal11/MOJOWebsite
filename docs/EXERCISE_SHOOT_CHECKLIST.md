Exercise Shoot Checklist

Framing and Style
- Neutral, uncluttered background; good, even lighting
- Camera locked off; primary angle 16:9 landscape; optional square crop
- Demonstrate 1–2 reps at controlled tempo; show both sides for unilateral
- Wear high-contrast apparel; avoid noisy patterns

Deliverables per exercise
- Poster image (webp/jpg), 16:9 and 1:1 preferred
- Short loop (3–5s) MP4/WebM, <= 1.5MB, muted
- How‑to video (10–20s), with captions (VTT)
- Text: instructions (3–6 steps), coaching cues, common mistakes, safety notes

Metadata to capture
- Movement family, primary/secondary muscles, modality, difficulty, unilateral
- Required/optional equipment, suitable environments (home/gym/outdoors)
- Variants: regressions, progressions, alternatives (IDs or names)

File naming (suggestion)
- Raw: exercise-media/uploads/{slug}/{slug}_raw.mp4
- Poster: exercise-media/processed/{slug}/poster.webp
- Loop: exercise-media/processed/{slug}/loop.mp4
- HLS: exercise-media/processed/{slug}/hls/index.m3u8

Review
- Safety and form approved by coach
- Captions verified; alt text added for posters
- Publish only after metadata and media are complete
